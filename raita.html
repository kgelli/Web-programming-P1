RAITA

INGREDIENTS

ONIONS

1 1/2

CHILLIS

3

SALT

2 tbsp

CORAINDER

1 1/2 stems

CUCUMBER

1 peeled

CURD

1 1/2 cup

JEERA

1 1/2 tbsp

STEPS

(10 MINUTES)

  • Add peeled cucumber, chopped onion, coriander, yoghurt, jeera , salt to a large bowl.
  • Mix all ingredients well until the raita is thoroughly combined. Top with extra coriander.
  • Chill and Serve.

Raita, a traditional Indian condiment, is made by blending yogurt with spices, along with finely chopped or grated vegetables and fruits. Raita is often paired with biryani to balance the richness and spiciness of the dish.

The cool and refreshing nature of raita, with its yogurt base and refreshing ingredients like cucumber or mint, helps to counteract the heat of the spices in the biryani, providing a harmonious and complementary dining experience.
